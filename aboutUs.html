About us

关于我们

GoEasy is a web realtime message push platform built by GOEASY LIMITED, which aims to help developers complete the integration of web message communication in their own projects in the simplest way and short time.
GoEasy team was founded in 2011, as a leading SAAS infrastructure services provider, is committed to provide developers with a simple, stable and efficient basic services, to help developers reduce the cost, shorten the delivery cycle, allowing developers to focus on the core business of their product.
成都优望软件有限公司, 是一家致力于通过云技术，为开发者提供简单，易用、高效的基础设施服务，帮助开发者降低项目的开发成本，缩短项目的交付周期，让开发者能聚焦于项目本身的核心业务。
作为GoEasy官方授权的中文社区服务商，优望软件将通过专业，热诚的服务为GoEasy中文社区的的开发者提供技术支持和相关咨询服务。
